THEORY 01 · KAPLAN & KAPLAN (1989–2024)

Attention Restoration Theory

Coding, studying, and digital navigation all consume Directed Attention — a top-down, metabolically expensive cognitive mode. After ~8 hours, your prefrontal cortex is depleted. Result: DAF (Directed Attention Fatigue). Nature environments uniquely engage Involuntary Attention — effortless soft fascination — allowing the directed system to fully reboot. 2024 ERP studies confirm this with measurable increases in N2 amplitude during nature exposure.

THEORY 02 · ROGER ULRICH · EVOLUTIONARY BIOLOGY

Your nervous system has a genetically hardwired positive-affective response to non-threatening natural environments. This triggers immediate reduction in heart rate, blood pressure, and cortisol secretion. In contrast, social media maintains your HPA axis in a state of perpetual low-grade arousal. Stepping into Sanjay Van isn't a luxury — it's a zero-cost, zero-side-effect pharmacological intervention against academic anxiety.
